--- title: Experimental Features layout: wiki-page ---
This wiki page describes some features of HTCondor that are designated "Experimental". This means that in future version of HTCondor their behavior, configuration, or presence may change.

List of experimental features:

  • ExperimentalCustomPrintFormats
  • Overlapping job execution with output transfer: a new job can begin executing on a slot while output file transfer of the previous job happens
  • ExperimentalPandaMonitor
  • ExperimentalSupportForPeriodicCheckpointingInVanillaUniverse
  • ExperimentalCondorAnnex
