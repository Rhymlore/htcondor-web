CVSTrac Ticket State

The default ticket states are (at the database level) new , review , defer , active , fixed , tested , and closed .

While it is certainly possible to change them, note that internally CVSTrac interprets states starting with the characters "n" or "a" as active and those starting with "f", "t" or "c" as fixed .
