This means overriding the list of authentication methods (which includes SciTokens by default) by setting SEC_DEFAULT_AUTHENTICATION_METHODS to all the methods you would actually like to use. To simply remove SciTokens, set it to "FS,TOKEN,KERBEROS,GSI,SSL". full_details: Embargoed until future notice. --- {% include vulnerability.html %}
